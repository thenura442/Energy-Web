Geothermal Energy

What is Geothermal Energy?
Geothermal energy is the thermal energy in the Earth's crust which originates from the formation of the planet and from radioactive decay of materials in currently uncertain but possibly roughly equal proportions.To produce power from geothermal energy, wells are dug a mile deep into underground reservoirs to access the steam and hot water there, which can then be used to drive turbines connected to electricity generators.
Uses of Geothermal Energy?
Geothermal energy use can be divided into three categories:
  • direct-use applications
  • geothermal heat pumps (GHPs)
  • electric power generation
