Wave Energy

What is Wave Eneryg?
The conveyance and collection of energy by ocean surface waves is known as wave energy . The captured energy is subsequently used for a variety of tasks, including electricity generation water desalination and water pumping. Wave energy is a sort of renewable energy that is projected to be the world's largest ocean energy resource. Wave energy is produced using a variety of technologies. Absorbers attenuators oscillation water columns overtopping and the inverted-pendulum device are the five primary types of technology used.With a buoy abosorbers harvest energy from the rise and fall of the waves. After the energy is retrieved a linear or rotary generator is used to convert it to electrical energy.
Uses of Wave Eneryg?
