ABOUT US
Originally, Energyish organization began with the aim of providing rural areas with sustainable and affordable energy sources like Solar Energy. Energyish is a non-Profitable organization which was founded a few years back with only 4 people it has now expanded to a numerous number of supporters worldwide. Energyish is a completely virtually based organization. In the past few years, our team had successfully completed some project regarding a program of providing some solar panel systems to the rural poor people in Sri Lanka who do not have access to electricity and also, we had donated some electricity equipment’s that are useful for their daily life carrier. These are some of our projects that we have accomplished with the kind hearted from people around the world.
OUR VISION
Our vision is to making the needy tomorrow bright.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is to develop the methodology to provide electricity on free of charge those who are belonged to low income category.
